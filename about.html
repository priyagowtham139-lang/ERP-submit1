About Us

Built by people tired of switching tabs.

Corevo started as an internal tool to stop one warehouse team from re-typing the same order into four different systems. It's now the operating system for thousands of growing companies.

Home / About
Our story

From spreadsheet chaos to one platform.

2015

Founded in Bengaluru

Started building a shared CRM and Sales tool for a single logistics client.

2018

Inventory & Accounting Launch

Expanded into a full operations suite after customer demand for stock and finance tools.

2021

10,000 Companies

Crossed 10,000 active companies running daily operations on Corevo.

2025

Global Expansion

Opened support hubs in three time zones to serve customers in 120+ countries.

Our Mission

To give every growing business one place to run operations, instead of ten disconnected tools.

Our Vision

A world where switching business software is as easy as turning on a new app, not a year-long migration.

Our Values

Simplicity over feature bloat, transparency in pricing, and support that actually understands your workflow.
